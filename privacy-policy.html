What We Collect

We may collect information that you provide directly, including your name, phone number, service address, ZIP code, and details about your drain or sewer issue when you call or submit a request for service.

We also collect limited technical information automatically, such as IP address, browser type, device details, referring page, pages viewed, timestamps, and general location derived from IP address. We may use cookies, local storage, and similar tools to remember preferences and measure site performance.

How We Use It

  • To connect you with independent drain cleaning or sewer service providers in your area.
  • To respond to inquiries, provide support, and improve response times.
  • To operate, secure, and maintain the website.
  • To analyze traffic, marketing performance, and call activity.
  • To comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud or abuse.

Sharing

We are a lead generation service and may share your request details with independent service providers, call routing partners, analytics providers, hosting vendors, and other vendors that help us operate the site and deliver service leads.

We do not sell sensitive personal information. If a disclosure qualifies as a sale or sharing under applicable law, you may exercise the rights described below.

California Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you may have the right to request access to the personal information we collect, request deletion of information subject to legal exceptions, request correction of inaccurate information, and request details about categories of information disclosed for business purposes.

You also may request to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information, where applicable. We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights. To submit a request, contact us using the information below and include enough detail for us to verify your request.

Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to remember basic site preferences, improve performance, and understand how visitors use the website. Where analytics tools are used, data may be aggregated or pseudonymized.

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Blocking some cookies may affect certain site features or measurement tools.

Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to operate the website, fulfill lead requests, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and maintain business records. Retention periods may vary depending on the type of information and the purpose for which it was collected.

Children

This website is intended for adults seeking home service assistance and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information through this site, contact us so we can review and remove it where appropriate.

Third-Party Links

This site may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices, content, or policies of third-party sites. Review the privacy policies of any external site you visit.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. Your continued use of the site after an update means the revised policy will apply to future use of the site.
