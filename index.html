What causes frequent drain clogs?

Common causes include grease and food buildup in kitchen drains, hair and soap scum in bathroom drains, tree root intrusion in sewer lines, and flushing non-degradable items. Regular maintenance and periodic drain cleaning can prevent recurring blockages.

Is hydro jetting safe for older pipes?

Hydro jetting is safe for most pipe materials when performed by a licensed professional. A camera inspection is usually conducted first to assess the pipe condition. For very old or fragile pipes, your technician may recommend alternative cleaning methods to avoid damage.
